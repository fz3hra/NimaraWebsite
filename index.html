What is Nimara about?

Dipped in the shades of red, our website is a tool sine qua non for the functioning of our robot Nimara. Specifically, the colour depicts life, health, vigor and courage which sums up the latter's motive. This website of which doctors and nurses will be the users with the help of an email address and password available in the hospital's database, is designed to feed the machine with appropriate data. It will prompt the person to input the patient ID, patient Name, necessary equipments such as medicines and injections and the dose to be used. In turn, Nimara will accordingly bring all the materials needed to the patient and administer the injection. For the time being, Nimara will only transport required drugs. However, with adequate training and upgrading with AI, it will accurately perform injections. In the long run, it can also be used to tend the wounded at war.
